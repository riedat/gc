Re: I-140 Immigrant Petition of EB1 Extraordinary Ability - Dr. Kevin Yong

 

Dear Officer:

It is my pleasure to write this letter in support of Dr. Kevin Yong’s I-140 petition for EB1 Alien of Extraordinary Ability to obtain permanent resident status in the United States, and also providing the explanations that his current research and development work is truly outstanding.

I came to know Dr. Kevin Yong’s research work and his exceptional ability in my capacity as a director of EDMS (Electronic Document Management System) project at GE Power System. Dr. Yong is a leading researcher in EDMS at GE R&D Center. Since June 2006, Dr. Yong has been working on document management automation research and development for applications in engineering and government regulatory compliance, as a principal investigator.

GE Power System is the world’s leader in power system research and design. GE Power Systems concurrently manages many national and international power engineering projects, which may be located in numerous foreign countries. The EDMS project at GE is an important research and exploratory development of automating business processes and managing critical information.

To meet the industry’s strategic and mission requirements, such as quick Action Items response and appropriate Vendor Document (VENDOC) management, effective new process development and algorithm research are essential. These techniques are used to achieve the goal of having immediate retrieval capability of document in electronic form directly to an end-user’s desktop while maintaining document security. Dr. Kevin Yong’s recent demonstration of VENDOC process in EDMS proved his superior research abilities, and received approval of all evaluation experts coming from both GE and other organizations,.

EDMS research and development is also very important to the American people and government. In 2005, based on Library of Congress’ National Digital Library Program, the Digital Library Federation (DLF) was established by "fifteen of the nation's largest research libraries and archives to create, maintain, expand, and preserve a distributed collection of digital materials of our nation’s history and culture accessible to schools, libraries, and American’s home everywhere." In this program, how to maintain and preserve digital documents and records securely and permanently is a challenging research area. 

Dr. Kevin Yong’s current research and development project in the field of automated document management will have direct contribution to save our nation’s contemporary and historical information, and his research result was awarded "Best Research Paper" in recent EDMS international conference at Paris, French.

An example that U.S. government agencies can directly use Dr. Kevin Yong’s research results is the TVA (Tennessee Valley Authority) projects at GE, which are the construction of STAG combined-cycle power plants at New Johnsonville, TN and Gallatin, TN. These two TVA projects are critical to continuous economical development in seven southeastern states of United Stated. For these TVA projects, Dr. Kevin Yong’s extraordinary research ability made a significant contribution, including assigning and tracking TVA’s Action Items, providing a timely and complete Action Item response to TVA, and managing various types of TVA documents inside EDMS.

Dr. Kevin Yong’s outstanding research and development work in EDMS also enables GE Power Systems to keep on being competitive in the global market, while making their products and services better and faster. According to GE’s 2008 annual report, "GE Power Systems won $12.5 billion contracts, and power plant orders increase 120% for the year," driven by record demand for gas turbines and maintenance services in the international market.

Dr. Kevin Yong earned his Master degree and Ph.D degree in Electrical Engineering from Cornell University. Before coming to United States, he had 8 years industry experience as a research engineer in computer-aided design and simulation in China. He has published eleven papers with high academic quality in scientific journals and international conferences.

Dr. Kevin Yong is an accomplished researcher whose skills and knowledge in EDMS and information technology is exceptional. The EDMS research and development is one of the key technological areas in which the U.S. must dominate in order to maintain the high-tech strength of the nation in the global market. 

Dr. Kevin Yong has extraordinary research ability, and his continuous research work will provide more contributions and prosperity to our nation. I believe that this letter will serve to strengthen Dr. Kevin Yong’s case, and that his petition will be granted. If there are any questions, please feel free to contact me.

Sincerely

 

 

Jeremy Collins
Director of EDMS Project
GE Power Systems
